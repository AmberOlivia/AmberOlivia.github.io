R3LIVE demo
========
This is a demo video of R3LIVE using Livox AVIA and ZED 2i sensors, mounted with the 3D printed handheld.
[R3LIVE test demo](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1vJIit0vb6lQK_rDnL5EXFxTfqKqfB0fq/view?usp=sharing)

- Synchronization is vital for sensor fusion.
> - Temporal synchronization requires time stamp alignment.  
> - Spatial synchronization requires calibration: camera calibration for intrinsic parameters; camera & LiDAR joint calibration for relative pose.
