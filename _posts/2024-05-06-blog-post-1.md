During indoor testing, tracking is lost when turing toward a blank wall. Failure notes:
- Relies heavily on ORB features, which struggle in low-texture or featureless environments like blank walls, and in poor lighting conditions like dark corridors.  
- Rapid camera movements can cause motion blur, leading to increased drift and tracking failures.
- It assumes a mostly static environment. Performs poorly in scenes with significant moving objects.
